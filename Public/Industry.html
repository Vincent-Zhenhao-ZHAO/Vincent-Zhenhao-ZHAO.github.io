The Underwriter will be approached by an Insurance broker. The Insurance broker provides a slip for the Underwriter to get the Underwriter to sign a percentage line of coverage, for that percentage cut of the premium.

Underwriter collates information from in-house actuarial team, claims history, operational infromation, Line of Business capacity; collating infromation together, makes a decison on whether to Undewrite risk and what percentage willing to write.

Having made a decision,there is still some negotiations on both the broker and Underwriter's side, as the broker may change the signed line to a different percentage to meet the requirements of the client, or there are endorsements that need to be added to the slip. The Underwriter makes the decision whether to accept the written line or reject accordingly.

Underwriting Testimonial

When a hazard turns into a claim, the client informs the appropriate broker, whom informs the loss adjusters and relevant Insurers as per the slip.

The loss adjusting agency, operates as a seperate entity, providing information on the severity of the cause and enables isngihts as to the amount of money that will have to be paid, according to the slip's breakdown.

Claims specialists are informed of the loss adjuster's reports, dissect the slip and are able to quantify AGCS's share of the pay-out. Systems are updated, recorded updated and the claim is paid appropraitely to the client.
