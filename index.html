Begin your journey with us

Here at Allianz Global Corporate and Speciality (AGCS) we do things differently. We specialise in risks that span the globe and makes real world differences. Our Early careers offerings aim to provide you with the toolkit to learn about truly global risks and help deliver solutions to the variety of sitations. Joining us will expand your horizons and nurture your career. Regardless of your background, we want people who are driven, curious and are excited by being a player on the world-stage, so apply and grow into a leading insurance professional.

BENEFITS OF WORKING AT AGCS

Card title

AGCS as the corporate and speciality branch of Allianz, is a global business with an outreach to over 200 countries and territories. AGCS is one of the few global insurers that exclusively focusses on high severity, low frequency risks. Combined with the wider Allianz group, we deliver the best in-class financial solutions for our ever growing diverse range of clients.

Card title

We care deeply about the professional development of our employees. AGCS provides funding and study leave for employees to acquire professional certifications, such as the CII for underwriters or operations, or PRINCE2/PMP for IT. We firmly believe in maximising our professional support for our employees to succeed in the ever-competitve Insurance landscape.

Card title

Here at AGCS we have a 60-40 hybrid working approach, where employees can coordinate with their team and choose which days they come in. We firmly believe in giving our employees choice. Choice and flexibility for our employees is what makes us a great employer, particularly for early careers. We have our own new proprietary new Ways of Working (WOW), setting out Global Minimum Standards, putting employee well-being at the forefront.
